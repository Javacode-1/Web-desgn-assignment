BIO

Hello! I'm Michael Ampomah, a BSc. Computer Engineering student at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology. Raised by an amazing family made up of my Parents and 3 siblings, I'm grateful for the values and support that have shaped me.

I was fortunate to attend the prestigious Osei Kyeretwie Senior High School in Kumasi, Ghana.

Now, I'm excited to the explore the world of computer engineering, fueled my love for electronics, creativity and curiosity.

WHY COMPUTER ENGINEERING?

I am remimded of the time I decided to pursue Computer Engineering. In fact it was a tough decision to make but all the same it was an importantr one too.

Growing up, I was fascinated by the rapid revolution of technology and its profound impact on our daily lives. From smartphones to laptops doing all the seemingly magical stuff.

My curioity led me to explore the field of Computer Science but I later discovered that computer engineering was meant for me; the blend of hands-on electronic projects with the headache of debugging codes was the best I could ever dream of.
